Philippine BIR Alphanumeric Tax Codes — Expanded & Final, Individual & Corporate

Reflects the RR 11-2018 (TRAIN) schedule as amended through RMO 46-2025 (October 27, 2025). Always verify against the BIR Alphalist module and the latest issuances before filing.

How to read these codes

Two different uses of the letters cause most of the confusion:

ContextWhat the prefix means
Tax-type code (on the return / form) WC = Compensation · WE = Expanded · WF = Final · WB = Business/Percentage · WV = VAT
ATC payee prefix (inside EWT & FWT) WI = payee is an Individual · WC = payee is a Corporation / juridical entity
WI### = Individual payee WC### = Corporate payee

Withholding on Compensation & adjacent withholding

Compensation (tax type "WC"): not broken into WI/WC payee ATCs — computed from the BIR graduated withholding tax tables (RR 11-2018, as adjusted), remitted monthly via 1601-C and reconciled annually via 1604-C. The payee is always an individual employee, so there is no corporation/individual split.
